Ricotta Gnocchi

Description

Easy recipe for a light and pillowy homemade gnocchi

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp fresh cracked pepper

Steps

  1. Prep water: bring large stockpot of salted water to boil
  2. Drain Ricotta: soak as much moisture from ricotta between 2 paper towels. transfer cheese to large mixing bowl
  3. Mix dough ingredients: add egg yolks and stir to combine. Add flour, parm, salt and pepper. stir until evenly combined. dough will be a bit moist and possibly sticky, but hold together well. Add a few tablespoons of flour if too wet
  4. Roll out and cut dough: roll to round disk, Cut into 8 even pie wedges. using hands, gently roll out each wedge into even log. Cut into bite size pieces. lightly toss and coat with flour
  5. Boil Gnocchi: transfer to boiling water. Once floating, remove and drain.
  6. Serve: serve immediately tossed with favorite sauce
